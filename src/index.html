Arrangements for the G9-G12 Whole-day Face-to-Face Classes

The student vaccination rates for G9, G10, G11 and G12 have exceeded 90%, thus fulfilling the EDB's requirement for whole-day face-to-face classes.

Date: From Tue 13th September 2022 Onwards

Morning Session
(7 Periods)		 Lunch Time Afternoon Session
(2 Periods)

Students may refer to the revised time-tables on PKC Zoom Timetable.

Lunch Arrangements

  1. The seats / tables at the canteen will be separated by partition boards.
  2. Students are required to have their lunch at the designated area inside the canteen.
  3. Students have to put on their masks immediately after lunch.

Arrangements for Face-to-Face Classes

All kindergartens, primary and secondary schools will continue to have half-day face-to-face classes for the time being in the 2022/23 school year.

For every school day, parents are required to report to our school their children's RAT results (together with body temperature) using the function of the eClass platform by 8:10 a.m.
